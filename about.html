We Are What We Do: Passion in Every Action

What we do?

At Research Materials, our primary mission is to provide researchers with a seamless and reliable source for their critical needs. We specialize in supplying research materials with detailed manufacturing specifications and certified compositions. We understand that accuracy and precision are paramount in research, which is why we ensure that our materials meet the highest quality standards.

In addition to research materials, we also offer a wide range of laboratory equipment and machinery, catering to both simple and complex needs. Whether you require basic lab instruments or specialized machinery for advanced experiments, we have you covered. With Research Materials, you can trust that you're not only receiving the finest research materials and equipment but also the support and expertise of a team that shares your passion for scientific exploration.

Who we are?

We are Research Materials, a division of Mechlergy Private Limited. Founded by a team of accomplished IITians in 2021, we have a profound appreciation for the significance of research materials in various scientific and engineering endeavors. At Research Materials, we recognize that the success of any research project depends on the quality and precision of the materials used, whether it's Aluminum, Magnesium, Steel, Nano-Particles, or any other essential component.

Understanding the importance of detailed information about material processing and exact standard compositions, we provide researchers and industry professionals with comprehensive data and high-quality materials. We believe that transparent and precise information is the foundation of successful research and development.

We also understand the challenges faced by buyers in sourcing research materials and laboratory equipment. Often, these items are scattered across various suppliers, making it inconvenient for researchers to acquire everything they need in one place. Research Materials addresses this issue by offering a one-stop solution for all your research material needs. Our commitment to being research-friendly is embedded in our DNA. Each of our directors has a professional background in the field, ensuring that we not only meet but exceed the expectations of our clients. Whether you are a scientist, engineer, or a professional working on cutting-edge research projects, Research Materials is here to simplify your sourcing process and provide you with the high-quality materials and equipment you need to advance your work. Your research journey begins here, with Research Materials.
